About me

I'm a roboticist and research scientist passionate about making positive impacts through technology and science. I have a broad range of experience from government and academic research (Sandia National Laboratories, US Dept. of Energy; Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems, Germany) to industry (Lockheed Martin; Doron Precision Systems), and have communicated my work to audiences in high-impact journals, conferences, and public presentations. I am excited to leverage my skillsets in both computational and experimental methods to solve new robotics problems.

Soft Robotic Shape Display

A smart robotic device giving a sense of touch

Published in Nature Communications, my coauthors and I developed an advanced 100-actuator, 100-sensor system.

Potential field control algorithm

Harnessing gradient descent for complex object manipulation

I developed and experimentally validated the performance of a control algorithm using our robotic shape display which can drive complex trajectories of surface objects.

6-DOF manipulator simulation

Documenting manipulator control strategies

I simulate control of a 6-DOF robot arm in Gazebo Sim through two methods: inverse kinematic control, and reinforcement learning.
